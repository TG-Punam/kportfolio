🛒GROCO-SHOP WEBSITE🛍️


KEY HIGHLIGHTS OF THE PROJECT INCLUDE :


🔎 Easily & Impressive Search Bar, Cart & Login: I have implemented a toggle functionality that allows seamless access to the search bar, cart, and user login sections.

🛍️ Efficient Product Management: Through the effective utilization of event listeners, I have ensured the smooth addition and removal of products from the list, optimizing the overall shopping process.

📸 Interactive Slider: By incorporating left and right arrows, users can effortlessly navigate between previous and next slides, enabling them to explore various offerings in an engaging manner.

📱 Responsive Design: The website is fully responsive, ensuring optimal functionality and visual appeal across different devices and screen sizes.
